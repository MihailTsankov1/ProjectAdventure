Stay Safe

Always prioritize safety when embarking on an adventure. Avoid restricted areas, unsafe zones, or private property. If the location seems unsafe, you may skip it.

Respect Nature and Communities

Leave no trace during your adventure. Take care of the environment, respect wildlife, and be courteous to local communities.
